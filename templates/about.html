Kumudhini Silks is a self-built brand born from the vision and perseverance of Mrs. Kumudhini V., who began her journey as a young girl supporting her family by selling sarees after school. Each saree she sold carried her dedication, each customer her growing confidence.

What began as a modest livelihood has flourished into one of Bengaluru's leading saree destinations, known for authenticity, artistry, and trust.

For over 35 years, Kumudhini Silks has remained rooted in the values of hard work, honesty, and heritage - creating a brand that celebrates women's strength and South Indian craftsmanship.

Today, Kumudhini Silks is more than a name; it is a legacy woven from the threads of resilience and refinement. Our founder's belief that "true beauty lies in authenticity" continues to inspire every collection we curate.

Each saree in our store - whether a royal Kancheepuram or a delicate tissue weave - carries the same spirit that started it all: pure, graceful, and enduring.
